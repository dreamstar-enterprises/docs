ETL - Extract, Transform, & Load

ETL - Extract, Transform, & Load

Use data connectivity and preparation technologies to seamlessly import and perform the extract, transform, and loading of data. Users can spend up to 80% of their time on data preparation, delaying the time to analysis and decision making. ETL is designed to help address these challenges.
