Exit Interview Measures

The following measures are shown:

  1. Response Rates: by Quarter
  2. Response Rates: by Job Grade
  3. Response Rates: by Department
  4. No of Exit Interviews: by Department
  5. Leaving Score: by Leaving Reason, detailed by Push or Pull
  6. Leaving Score: by Quarter, detailed by Push or Pull
  7. Leaving Score: by Department, detailed by Push or Pull
  8. Leaving Score: by Job Grade, detailed by Push or Pull
  9. Leaving Score: by Leaving Reason, detailed by Quarter
  10. Leaving Score: by Leaving Reason, detailed by Department and Job Grade
  11. Leaving Score: by Quarter, detailed by Department and Job Grade
  12. Leaving Score: by Department, detailed by Quarter and Job Grade
  13. Leaving Score: by Job Grade, detailed by Department and Quarter
  14. Leaving Score: by Leaving Reason and Quarter - Table
  15. Leaving Score: by Leaving Reason and Job Grade, and Department - Table
  16. Other Exit Questions: A variety of other exit interview responses

Model Extensions

Possible extensions to this model, could include:

  • Inclusion of ‘Freelancers’ to all datasets, to help distinguish from ‘Permanent’ staff, e.g. by adding an extra field called ‘Freelancers vs Permanent’.
  • Freelance records could be automatically linked and created from their agency Timesheet submissions.
  • Inclusion of ‘Gender’ as an additional field to all datasets
  • Adjustment of calculations accordingly.

Measures:

Exit Interviews

DAX 
Exit Interviews = 
CALCULATE(COUNT(Leavers[Name]), Leavers[Reason for Leaving (3 points)] <> BLANK())

Response Rates %

DAX 
Response Rates % = 
DIVIDE([Exit Interviews], COUNT(Leavers[Name]), BLANK())

Calculated Columns:

Leavers to Date

DAX 
Leavers to Date = 
CALCULATE (
    COUNT ( Leavers[Name] ),
    FILTER (
        SUMMARIZE ( Leavers, Leavers[Department] ),
        Leavers[Department] = 'Average Headcount'[Department]
    )
)

Leavers to Date (Managed)

DAX 
Leavers to Date (Managed) = 
CALCULATE (
    COUNT ( Leavers[Name] ),
    FILTER (
        SUMMARIZE (
            FILTER ( Leavers, Leavers[Tracking] = "Managed" ),
            Leavers[Department],
            Leavers[Tracking]
        ),
        Leavers[Department] = 'Average Headcount'[Department]
    )
)

Leavers to Date (Unmanaged)

DAX 
Leavers to Date (Unmanaged) = 
CALCULATE (
    COUNT ( Leavers[Name] ),
    FILTER (
        SUMMARIZE (
            FILTER ( Leavers, Leavers[Tracking] = "Unmanaged" ),
            Leavers[Department],
            Leavers[Tracking]
        ),
        Leavers[Department] = 'Average Headcount'[Department]
    )
)

Managed Turnover % to date

DAX 
Managed Turnover % to date = 
DIVIDE('Average Headcount'[Leavers to Date (Managed)], 'Average Headcount'[Average Headcount per Month], BLANK())

Total Turnover % to date

DAX 
Total Turnover % to date = 
DIVIDE('Average Headcount'[Leavers to Date], 'Average Headcount'[Average Headcount per Month], BLANK())

Unmanaged Turnover % to date

DAX 
Unmanaged Turnover % to date = 
DIVIDE('Average Headcount'[Leavers to Date (Unmanaged)], 'Average Headcount'[Average Headcount per Month], BLANK())

Year & Month

DAX 
Year & Month = YEAR(Leavers[Leave Date]) & " - " & MONTH(Leavers[Leave Date])
